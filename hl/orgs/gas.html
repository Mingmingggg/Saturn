ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

Gakuen Anime Soshiki (The School of Anime) considers itself as a lively, fun, and diverse organization, being a community that has grown with its members' interests and hobbies in Japanese media and its culture. Members here have an open and accepting space to actively bond and share their love for different genres of anime, manga, video games, music, and other types of media.

Mission

To become a well-known organization that creates an interactive and socially relevant community that aims to inspire, encourage, and enhance the talents and skills of Lasallians through immersion in Japanese Media Culture.

Vision

The organization envisions a community of people with common interests. Creating and strengthening the bond of each individual through activities, we envision to accomplish all projects of the organization.
